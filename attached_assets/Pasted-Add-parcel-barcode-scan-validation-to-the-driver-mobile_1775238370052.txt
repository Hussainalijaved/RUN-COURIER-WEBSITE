Add parcel barcode scan validation to the driver mobile app.

Important business rule:
- The barcode is NOT created by our system.
- The barcode already exists on the parcel/item if available.
- Driver scans the barcode printed on the collected item.
- The same barcode must be scanned again at delivery for the same item.

Required logic:

1. Pickup / Collection
- Add an optional “Scan Parcel Barcode” button on the collection screen.
- Scanning is optional.
- If the parcel has no barcode or driver does not scan one, the job can continue normally.
- If the driver scans a barcode at collection:
  - Save that barcode value in the job record in Supabase.
  - Mark the job as barcode_scanned_at_pickup = true.
  - Save pickup scan timestamp.
  - Show the scanned barcode in the driver app and admin panel.

2. Delivery
- If no barcode was scanned at collection:
  - Delivery can be completed normally without barcode scan.
- If a barcode was scanned at collection:
  - Driver MUST scan a barcode before completing delivery.
  - The delivery scan must EXACTLY match the barcode scanned at collection.
  - If barcode does not match, show error:
    “Scanned barcode does not match the parcel collected”
  - Block delivery completion until the correct barcode is scanned.

3. Validation rules
- Do not accept a different barcode at delivery.
- Do not allow delivery completion if pickup barcode exists but delivery barcode is missing.
- Do not allow delivery completion if delivery barcode is different from pickup barcode.
- No manual typing or manual override for barcode values.
- Barcode value must come only from actual scanner/camera scan.

4. Rescan / replacement at collection
- If driver already scanned a barcode at collection and scans another one by mistake:
  - Show confirmation:
    “Replace previously scanned parcel barcode?”
  - Only replace if driver confirms.
- Save only one barcode per job.

5. Database / Supabase
Add or use fields in jobs table:
- pickup_barcode (text)
- delivery_barcode (text)
- barcode_scanned_at_pickup (boolean)
- barcode_verified_at_delivery (boolean)
- pickup_barcode_scan_time (timestamp)
- delivery_barcode_scan_time (timestamp)

6. Admin dashboard
Show in admin job details:
- Pickup barcode
- Delivery barcode
- Barcode scanned at pickup: Yes/No
- Barcode verified at delivery: Yes/No
- Pickup scan time
- Delivery scan time

7. Driver app UI
Add barcode section in job details:
- At collection: “Scan Parcel Barcode” button
- After scan: show scanned barcode and status “Barcode captured”
- At delivery: if pickup barcode exists, show “Barcode verification required before delivery completion”
- After successful delivery scan: show “Barcode verified”

8. Persistence
- Barcode data must be saved permanently in Supabase.
- If app closes or refreshes, the scanned barcode must still appear.
- Same barcode data must be visible in both mobile app and website admin panel.

9. Platform requirements
- Must work on both iOS and Android in Expo.
- Use device camera barcode scanner already available in the app if possible.
- Do not change any other job flow or pricing logic.

Final expected behavior:
- Barcode scan is optional at collection.
- If no barcode is scanned at collection, no barcode is required at delivery.
- If barcode is scanned at collection, the same barcode becomes mandatory at delivery.
- Delivery cannot be completed unless the exact same parcel barcode is scanned again.
