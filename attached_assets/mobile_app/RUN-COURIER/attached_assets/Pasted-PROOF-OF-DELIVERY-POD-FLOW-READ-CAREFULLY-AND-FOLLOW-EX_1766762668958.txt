PROOF OF DELIVERY (POD) FLOW

READ CAREFULLY AND FOLLOW EXACTLY — PRODUCTION APP

You are working on a production courier driver mobile app (React Native + Expo) called Run Courier.

🎯 GOAL

Implement a professional Proof of Delivery (POD) flow that allows drivers to take multiple photos, validate them, and complete the job only after POD is provided.

This must work reliably for real courier operations.

🚫 NOT ALLOWED

❌ Do NOT allow delivery completion without POD

❌ Do NOT limit to only 1 photo

❌ Do NOT auto-complete delivery without driver confirmation

❌ Do NOT use temporary local images only (must upload)

✅ REQUIRED POD BEHAVIOUR
1️⃣ POD MODAL / SCREEN

When the driver taps “Complete Delivery”:

Open a bottom sheet or full screen modal titled:

“Proof of Delivery”

2️⃣ PHOTO CAPTURE (MANDATORY)

Inside POD screen:

Allow the driver to take 1 or more photos

Use device camera only (no gallery upload unless explicitly enabled)

Show thumbnails of captured photos

Allow:

➕ Add more photos

❌ Remove a photo before submitting

Minimum requirement:

At least 1 photo required

No maximum limit (or set safe limit like 5–10)

3️⃣ OPTIONAL POD DATA (RECOMMENDED)

Include optional fields:

Recipient name (text)

Delivery notes (text)

Signature (optional – can be phase 2)

These fields are optional, photos are mandatory.

4️⃣ VALIDATION RULES (STRICT)

Before enabling “Complete Delivery” button:

✅ At least 1 photo exists

✅ Photos uploaded successfully

❌ If upload fails → show error, do NOT complete job

Disable button until conditions are met.

5️⃣ UPLOAD LOGIC (IMPORTANT)

When submitting POD:

Upload images to storage (Supabase / S3 / Firebase)

Store:

jobId

driverId

image URLs

timestamp

GPS location (if available)

Only after successful upload:
➡️ Mark job as DELIVERED

6️⃣ UX REQUIREMENTS

Show upload progress indicator

Disable buttons while uploading

Show success confirmation:
“Delivery completed successfully”

7️⃣ BACKEND / DATA UPDATE

On success:

Update job status → DELIVERED

Save POD data linked to job

Lock job from further edits

8️⃣ FILE STRUCTURE (USE OR CREATE)

screens/driver/ProofOfDeliveryModal.tsx

components/PODImagePicker.tsx

services/podUploadService.ts

Keep logic reusable and clean.

9️⃣ FINAL CHECK (MANDATORY)

Before finishing, confirm:

Driver cannot complete delivery without photos

Multiple photos work correctly

Job status updates only after successful upload

UX feels professional and reliable
